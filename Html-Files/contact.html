Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with Sasnaka Sansada?

You can volunteer by filling out our volunteer application form on the Volunteer page. We have various opportunities available throughout the year.

What types of partnerships do you offer?

We offer various partnership opportunities including project collaborations, sponsorships, and long-term strategic partnerships.

How can I make a donation?

You can make donations through our secure online platform or by contacting our team directly for other donation methods.

What areas do you serve?

We serve communities across Sri Lanka with a focus on education, healthcare, and sustainable development initiatives.
