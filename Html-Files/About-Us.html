Our Story

The Beginning of a Movement

Founded in 1997 by a group of visionary young leaders, Sasnaka Sansada emerged from a shared commitment to create positive social change. What started as a small initiative has grown into a nationwide movement, touching lives across 25 districts in Sri Lanka.

Our founders believed in the power of youth to drive meaningful change in society. They envisioned an organization that would not just provide services but would empower communities through education, leadership, and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To empower Sri Lankan youth through innovative STEAM education, entrepreneurial opportunities, leadership development, and community service initiatives. We strive to create lasting positive change by:

  • Fostering innovation and technological literacy
  • Supporting youth entrepreneurship
  • Developing future leaders
  • Building sustainable communities

Our Vision

To be the leading youth-led organization in Sri Lanka, driving social transformation through:

  • A network of empowered youth leaders across all districts
  • Sustainable community development initiatives
  • Innovation hubs for STEAM education
  • Thriving youth entrepreneurship ecosystem

"Building a just and equitable society where every young person has the opportunity to thrive and contribute to positive change."
